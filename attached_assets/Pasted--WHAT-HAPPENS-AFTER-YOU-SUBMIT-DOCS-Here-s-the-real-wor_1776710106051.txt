🚀 WHAT HAPPENS AFTER YOU SUBMIT DOCS

Here’s the real-world timeline (you should show this in your app):

📬 STEP 1 — DEFENDANT IS NOTIFIED (Service of Process)

Timeline: 1–10 days after filing

Court or process server delivers your claim
Defendant officially receives it

👉 This is when pressure starts

⏳ STEP 2 — WAIT PERIOD

Timeline: ~10–30 days (varies by state)

During this time, 3 things can happen:

🟢 Outcome A — They pay or settle (COMMON)
Defendant contacts you
Offers payment or negotiation

👉 Case ends early (best outcome)

🟡 Outcome B — They ignore it
No response
No court appearance

👉 You can request a default judgment

🔴 Outcome C — They dispute it
They respond
Court schedules hearing

👉 You go to court (usually short + informal)

⚖️ STEP 3 — HEARING (if needed)

Timeline: 2–8 weeks after filing

What happens:

You present your timeline + evidence
Defendant responds
Judge decides

👉 Usually 10–15 minutes

🧾 STEP 4 — JUDGMENT
Court issues decision
If you win → you are awarded money
💰 STEP 5 — COLLECTION (IMPORTANT)

Winning ≠ getting paid

If they don’t pay:

wage garnishment
bank levy
payment plan
