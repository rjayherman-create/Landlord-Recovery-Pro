If you do it wrong:

If you do it right:

🧠 THE RIGHT APPROACH

Never say:

“You don’t have a case”

Say:

“Your case may not be strong enough to justify filing right now”

👉 That’s guidance, not a hard rejection.

🎯 HOW TO POSITION IT
🔥 BEST VERSION
<div className="bg-yellow-50 border border-yellow-200 p-4 rounded mt-4">

  <p className="text-yellow-800 font-semibold">
    Your case appears to be low strength
  </p>

  <p className="text-sm mt-2 text-yellow-700">
    Based on your property data and comparable homes, your assessment appears 
    close to market value. Filing may not result in meaningful savings.
  </p>

</div>
💥 ADD THIS (VERY IMPORTANT)
<p className="text-sm text-gray-600 mt-2">
  We only recommend filing when there is a strong likelihood of savings.
</p>

👉 Now you’re positioned as:

honest + data-driven + not trying to take their money
🚀 GIVE THEM OPTIONS (DON’T DEAD-END)

Never stop the flow completely.

Offer:

OPTION 1 — Continue anyway
<button className="text-blue-600 underline mt-3">
  Continue anyway
</button>
OPTION 2 — Check again later
<p className="text-sm text-gray-500 mt-2">
  Property values change — you may want to recheck in the future.
</p>
OPTION 3 — Improve case
<p className="text-sm text-gray-500 mt-2">
  Adding additional comparable properties may strengthen your case.
</p>
🧠 WHY THIS WORKS

User thinks:

“These guys could have taken my $99… but didn’t”

👉 That builds HUGE trust

💰 BUSINESS IMPACT

Counterintuitive truth:

Rejecting weak cases → increases revenue

Because:

Strong cases convert higher
Fewer refunds / complaints
Better reviews
More referrals
