How It Works

File Your Appeal in 4 Simple Steps

No lawyers, no 50% commissions. Just a clear, guided process from assessment to reduction.

Look Up Your Property

Enter your address and we auto-fill your parcel ID, assessed value, and tax history from NYS ORPS or county records.

Pull Comparable Sales

We fetch nearby sales from public databases, filtered by size and neighborhood, to build your evidence package.

Generate Your Forms

We pre-fill your official grievance form (RP-524 for NY and equivalent for NJ, TX, FL) — ready to sign and file.

File & Win

Submit to your county's Board of Assessment Review or online portal. We track deadlines and remind you every step.
